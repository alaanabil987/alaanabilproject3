Welcome

Welcome to GiGI COFFEE. We are a roastery set out to source, roast, and serve the best coffee our resources allow us. We showcase these coffees by roasting them lightly. We believe every cup of coffee has the power to make your world better. Join us to help make a difference and discover how rewarding it can be.

OUR PRODUCTION

Our roasted coffee, processed at our factory following 4 main steps

Storage & cleaning

Cleaning raw coffee beans from any impurities or strange materials, then coffee is ready for roasting

Roasting

Roasting takes place according to the roasting profile light , medium or dark roast as well as espresso roast.

Grinding

Al Orouba has a special grinding techniques to ensure TGA quality and to deliver the perfect coffee.

Packaging

For this process we use the latest packaging machines without any human interaction.
